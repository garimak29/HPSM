Trace syntax
1>" t on "- for turnig on the trace.
2>"ba se.call.process"-for putting a breakpoint , whenever we call a proess.
3> "c"-for run (till a breakpoint is reached)
4> "s"-for checking the trace line one by one . 
5>"d $L.process.name"-for seeking the process name 
6>"ba se.call.process"- again to remove the breakpoint.
7>"t off"-for turning off the trace.
